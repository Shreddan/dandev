The A-Bomb


Just to clarify... I'm not talking about an Atom Bomb...
I'm talking about Autism A word that when dropped into conversation, can make those you are talking to go quiet for a moment as if considering their words.
Autism is a very divisive topic, which makes sense seeing as Autism itself is a spectrum. On the one hand there is alot more support and awareness in terms of autism, especially when compared to even only 10 or 15 years ago.
However there still those that aren't sure what to think about it, and this is what (I think) causes the moment of silence during the conversation. I fully understand that for those that have no experience with Autism that it might be a difficult topic to talk about, especially in this day and age, when it feels like it can be so easy to offend people.
